Quizz-Buzz!

Quizz-Buzz is the biggest quiz competition held all over India once in a year during Independence day. Participants from all over India of different educational institutions and of different age groups participate in the event. This is initiative taken by Government of India to Inspire young minds out there. There are different areas available to choose. There will be three rounds held for three days. Students will be competing with other students of their age group only. There can be maximum of three members in a team. Let's go!!
